# 📥 Submissions

This folder is where **you** (the student) place your completed **Main Task** submission.

> ⚠️ You only need to submit the **Main Task** (Deploy the Sample App). The smaller lesson tasks are optional practice — **do not submit those**.

---

## 📋 What goes here

Create a folder named after your GitHub username and put all your **Main Task screenshots** inside it:

```
submissions/
└── <your-github-username>/
    ├── step1-minikube-start.png
    ├── step2-docker-env.png
    ├── step3-docker-build.png
    ├── step4-kubectl-apply.png
    ├── step5-get-pods.png
    ├── step6-app-in-browser.png
    ├── step7-scaled-pods.png
    └── notes.md            (optional)
```

One screenshot **per step** of the Main Task. See the [Submission Guidelines](../SUBMISSION.md) for the full step-by-step process.

---

## ✅ After adding your screenshots

1. Open a **Pull Request** to the **`main` branch of this repo** (the course repo — **not** your own fork's main).
2. Copy your **PR link**.
3. Open a [**Submission issue**](../../issues/new?template=submission.yml) and paste your PR link + details.

Once your PR is reviewed and your submission issue is **closed by the maintainer**, you'll receive your **🏅 completion badge** automatically as a comment on your issue.
